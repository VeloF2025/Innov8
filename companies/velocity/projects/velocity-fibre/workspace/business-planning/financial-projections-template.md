## üìù Template Usage Notes

### Customization Guidelines
1. **Industry-Specific Metrics**: Adjust KPIs for your industry
2. **Revenue Model**: Tailor revenue streams to your business
3. **Cost Structure**: Customize cost categories for your business
4. **Time Horizons**: Adjust projection periods as needed
5. **Currency**: Use your local currency

### Quality Checklist
- [ ] All assumptions are documented and justified
- [ ] Financial statements are internally consistent
- [ ] Growth rates are realistic and supportable
- [ ] Cash flow projections include working capital changes
- [ ] Sensitivity analysis covers key risk factors
- [ ] Funding requirements are clearly tied to milestones

### Best Practices
- **Be Conservative**: It's better to under-promise and over-deliver
- **Document Everything**: Keep clear records of all assumptions
- **Regular Updates**: Review and update projections regularly
- **Cross-Validation**: Validate projections against industry benchmarks
- **Professional Review**: Have a financial professional review your projections
