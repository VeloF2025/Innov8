## ðŸ”„ Contractor Field Operations Model

### **Pre-80% Activation Phase**

**Contractor Responsibilities:**
- Fiber installation
- ONT/router installation
- Initial customer setup
- Emergency repairs
- Network troubleshooting

**VeloCity Provides:**
- Network design & specs
- Supervision (part-time)
- Quality assurance
- Backhaul connectivity
- Support for escalations

**Cost Structure:** R60K/month per project (fixed)
- Contractor absorbs installation risk
- VeloCity absorbs customer acquisition risk (marketing budget separate)
- Incentive: Faster installations = faster to 80%

### **Post-80% Activation Phase (Zone-by-Zone Transition)**

**Shift to Performance Model:**
- R10 per activated ONT per month
- Example: 6K activated ONTs = R60K/month (same cost as fixed)
- Contractor incentive: More activations = more revenue
- VeloCity benefit: Link ops cost to actual customers

**Transition Timeline:**
- Project goes live â†’ 6 months to 40% activation
- Month 6-12 â†’ 40% to 60% activation (still on fixed R60K)
- Month 12-18 â†’ 60% to 80% activation (fixed contract holds)
- Month 18+ â†’ 80%+ activation (convert to R10/ONT model)
