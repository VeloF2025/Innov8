## üìã **Project Summary**

### **Project Description**
VeloCity FNO 2025 is a Build-Operate-Maintain fibre network infrastructure initiative targeting underserved South African township communities. The project focuses on deploying and operating essential digital infrastructure with verified market demand and conservative financial assumptions.

### **Project Objectives**
- **Primary Goal**: Deploy and operate 10,000 home fibre network infrastructure with R33.75 million investment
- **Secondary Goal**: Achieve 14.3% Year 1 yield scaling to 25%+ by Year 3
- **Long-term Goal**: Expand to multiple township deployments with shared operational efficiencies

### **Key Market Opportunities (Verified)**
- **Total Addressable Market**: 4.2 million township households
- **Current Fibre Coverage**: <10% in target townships vs 35% national average
- **Value Proposition**: 20-30% below market pricing for underserved segments
- **Investment Returns**: Sustainable yields through essential infrastructure assets

### **Key Stakeholders**
- **Investment Community**: Infrastructure funds, pension funds, sovereign wealth funds
- **Executive Team**: Llewelyn Hofmeyr (CEO), Lourens Kleynhans (CFO), with CTO/COO to be appointed
- **Strategic Partners**: Municipal governments, equipment vendors, construction contractors
- **Community Stakeholders**: Township leadership and local residents
