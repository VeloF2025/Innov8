Revenue Ramp-Up Model

VeloCity's financial model reflects realistic operational deployment with gradual ONT activation and tiered pricing as projects scale:

Project Deployment Timeline:
  • Months 1-2: Project setup and fiber deployment (R400K/month OPEX, no revenue)
  • Month 3+: Begin ONT activations at ~250-300 per week (~1,100/month)
  • Month 5: Project reaches operational breakeven (monthly revenue > monthly OPEX)
  • Month 10: Project reaches mature state at 80% penetration (8,000 ONTs) with R10/day pricing
  • Month 20: Cumulative payback achieved (total R48.3M investment recovered)
Tiered Pricing Model (All Active ONTs):
  • Months 3-4: R3.50 per ONT per day (capacity building phase)
  • Months 5-6: R5.00 per ONT per day (early maturity)
  • Months 7-8: R7.50 per ONT per day (scaling phase)
  • Month 9+: R10.00 per ONT per day (full maturity)
