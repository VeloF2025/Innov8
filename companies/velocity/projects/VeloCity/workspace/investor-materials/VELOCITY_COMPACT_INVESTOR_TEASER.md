## üèóÔ∏è **Build-Operate-Maintain Model**

### **FNO + ISP Integration (Closed-Access Network)**
VeloCity is both the Fiber Network Operator (FNO) AND the Internet Service Provider (ISP):
- **Complete Control:** Full value chain ownership from infrastructure to customer service
- **Competitive Advantage:** Control pricing, service quality, and customer experience
- **Revenue Optimization:** All subscriber revenue flows to VeloCity (not shared with wholesale partners)

### **Hub & Spoke Operational Architecture**
**Exceptional Efficiency Through Shared Services:**
- Regional hub managers oversee 4-5 projects each
- Shared technical operations team across hub
- Consolidated customer service center (1 agent per 1,000 customers)
- Centralized financial and administrative functions
- Single management layer scales across portfolio

**Staffing Efficiency:**
| Phase | Total Staff | Staff per 10K Homes | Projects Managed |
|-------|------------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Year 1** | 15-25 | 3.75-6.25 | 4 projects |
| **Year 2** | 45-50 | 3.75-4.17 | 12 projects |
| **Year 3** | 65-70 | 2.71-2.92 | 24 projects |

**Comparison to Industry Norms:**
- **VeloCity:** 1 employee per 3,429 homes (highly efficient)
- **Industry Standard:** 1 employee per 2,000-2,500 homes
- **Original Model (Archived):** 1 employee per 256 homes (unrealistic)

### **Contractor Field Operations Model**

**Two-Phase Cost Structure (Risk & Incentive Alignment):**

**Phase 1: Pre-80% Activation**
- Fixed cost: R60K/month per project
- Contractor absorbs all field labor, installation, repairs
- VeloCity provides: network specifications, quality assurance, backhaul connectivity, supervision
- Contractor Risk: Installation costs, project delays
- VeloCity Leverage: Customer acquisition and revenue focus

**Phase 2: Post-80% Activation (Performance-Based)**
- Cost: R10 per activated ONT per month
- Example: 6K ONTs = R60K/month (same cost but now performance-driven)
- Contractor Incentive: More activations = more revenue
- VeloCity Benefit: Operational costs scale with actual customer base

**Financial Impact:**
```
Year 1:  4 projects all pre-80% = R240K/month contractor costs
Year 2:  Mix of pre/post-80% = ~R720K/month
Year 3:  Most post-80% = ~R1.44M/month (24 projects at R10/ONT avg)
```
