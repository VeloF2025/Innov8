## ðŸ’¡ Strategic Insights

### 1. Single Project Dynamics
- **Only viable at 70%+ penetration AND R10.00/day minimum**
- Lower daily rates (R2.50-R7.50) are economically unviable regardless of penetration
- 6,000 ONT baseline is below profitability threshold across all daily rates
- Requires aggressive customer acquisition (70%+) for single-project sustainability

### 2. Portfolio Leverage
- **Shared OPEX savings are critical** - 15% â†’ 25% â†’ 35% reductions
- Multi-project model (Year 3: 24 projects) generates 28.9% yield vs 4.4% single project
- Economies of scale in management, technical staff, and regional operations
- Portfolio approach makes marginal single projects viable

### 3. Penetration Rate Criticality
- **60% penetration**: Every scenario loses money - not deployable
- **70% penetration**: Only viable at premium tier (R10/day) - minimum threshold
- **80% penetration**: Strong performance across tiers, ideal target

### 4. Project Size Advantage
- **20K door projects**: More operational leverage, 7.3% yield at 70% (vs 4.4% for 10K)
- **20K door projects**: Can tolerate lower penetration (69% breakeven)
- Larger projects reduce per-home operational costs through efficiency

### 5. Daily Rate Tiers Impact
- **R2.50-R7.50 tiers**: Economically unviable for any single project
- **R10.00/day tier**: Only viable tier, generates all profitable scenarios
- **Pricing strategy critical**: Must achieve premium tier adoption or face losses
- Suggests market segmentation by service level (basic vs premium)
