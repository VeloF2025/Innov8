## Cost Optimization Strategies

### Staffing Optimization
1. **Automation**: Reduce manual processes through system automation
2. **Outsourcing**: Non-core functions (HR, payroll, IT support)
3. **Remote Work**: Reduce office space requirements
4. **Training**: Cross-training to improve staff efficiency

### Maintenance Optimization
1. **Predictive Maintenance**: AI-driven fault prediction
2. **Standardization**: Use common equipment across network
3. **Partnerships**: Shared maintenance agreements with other FNOs
4. **In-house Capability**: Build internal expertise vs expensive contractors

### Technology Optimization
1. **Open Source**: Use open-source software where possible
2. **Cloud Services**: SaaS vs on-premise solutions
3. **Bulk Licensing**: Volume discounts on software licenses
4. **Energy Efficiency**: Green technology to reduce power costs

## OPEX Variance Analysis

### Inflation Assumptions
- **Salary Inflation**: 6% annually
- **General Inflation**: 5% annually
- **Energy Costs**: 8% annually
- **Software Licensing**: 3% annually

### Best Case Scenario (10% OPEX Reduction)
- **Drivers**: Efficient operations, technology automation, bulk purchasing
- **7-Year Total OPEX**: R923 million vs R1.03 billion base case
- **Impact**: 15% improvement in profitability

### Worst Case Scenario (15% OPEX Increase)
- **Drivers**: Regulatory delays, higher energy costs, staffing challenges
- **7-Year Total OPEX**: R1.19 billion vs R1.03 billion base case
- **Impact**: Delay in break-even by 12 months
