## üîÑ **Contractor Field Operations Model**

### **Pre-80% Activation Phase**
- Contractor handles all installation, setup, repairs
- Fixed cost: R60K/month per project
- VeloCity provides: specs, supervision, backhaul, QA
- Contractor risk: Installation costs, project delays
- VeloCity risk: Customer acquisition, revenue

### **Post-80% Activation Phase**
- Contractor shifts to performance-based model
- Cost: R10 per activated ONT per month
- Example: 6K ONTs = R60K (same as fixed, but now tied to performance)
- Incentive: More activations = more revenue for contractor
- VeloCity benefit: Ops cost scales with actual customers

### **Financial Impact**
```
Year 1:  4 projects, all pre-80% = 4 √ó R60K = R240K/month
Year 2:  Mix of pre/post-80% = ~R720K/month (average R60K per project)
Year 3:  Most post-80% = ~R1.44M/month (24 projects at R10/ONT avg)
```
