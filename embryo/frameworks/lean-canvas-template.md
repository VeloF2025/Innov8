## üéØ Lean Canvas Overview - 15 Minutes

### **What is Lean Canvas?**
The Lean Canvas is a 1-page business model template that helps you deconstruct your idea into its fundamental assumptions. It's adapted from the Business Model Canvas and specifically designed for startups to quickly map out, test, and iterate their business model.

### **Why Use Lean Canvas?**
- **Speed**: Complete your business model in under 2 hours
- **Focus**: Forces prioritization of what really matters
- **Action-Oriented**: Emphasizes problems and solutions over features
- **Testable**: Each block represents a testable hypothesis
- **Iterative**: Easy to update as you learn from customers

### **The 9 Building Blocks**
1. **Problem**: Customer pain points you're solving
2. **Customer Segments**: Who you're creating value for
3. **Unique Value Proposition**: Why you're different and worth buying
4. **Solution**: Your product/service and key features
5. **Channels**: How you'll reach your customers
6. **Revenue Streams**: How you make money
7. **Cost Structure**: What your costs are
8. **Key Metrics**: How you measure success
9. **Unfair Advantage**: What makes you hard to copy

### **Time Estimates**
- **First Pass**: 1-2 hours
- **Research & Validation**: 2-3 hours
- **Refinement**: 1-2 hours
- **Total**: 4-7 hours
