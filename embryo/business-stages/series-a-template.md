## üìù Template Usage Notes

### Customization Guidelines
1. **Be specific with metrics** - use actual numbers and data
2. **Show growth trajectory** - demonstrate momentum and progress
3. **Address scalability** - show how you'll grow 10x
4. **Include competitive analysis** - demonstrate market understanding
5. **Detail use of funds** - be specific about how capital will be used

### Quality Checklist
- [ ] All financial projections are realistic and supported
- [ ] Market analysis includes current data and trends
- [ ] Team section demonstrates depth and experience
- [ ] Risk analysis is thorough and mitigation strategies are clear
- [ ] Use of funds is detailed and justified
- [ ] Growth strategy is ambitious but achievable
