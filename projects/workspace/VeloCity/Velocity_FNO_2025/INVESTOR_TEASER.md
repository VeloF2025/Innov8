## üíº **BUSINESS MODEL**

### **Build & Transfer Solutions**
1. **Project Identification**: Target rural/low-income areas with high demand
2. **Financing**: Self-fund entire infrastructure deployment
3. **Execution**: Turnkey delivery from civil works to network handover
4. **Transfer**: Sell completed network to FNO partners or retain operations

### **Revenue Streams**
- **Build & Transfer Fees**: One-time project revenue (R2,500-R4,000 per home passed)
- **Maintenance Contracts**: Ongoing network operations revenue
- **Consulting Services**: Pre-deployment planning and design
- **Partnership Income**: Joint venture revenue sharing

### **Competitive Advantages**
- **Financial Independence**: No equity dilution or debt requirements
- **Proven Execution**: Track record with major telecom projects
- **Rural Focus**: Underserved market with high demand and limited competition
- **Turnkey Capability**: End-to-end project delivery expertise
- **Enhanced Commercial Capability**: Strong commercial strategy and partnership development
